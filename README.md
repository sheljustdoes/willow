# Willow Care Co.
### Precision Care Navigation · Florida

> *What happens when a nurse gets tired of watching families drown in a system that wasn't designed for them.*

---

## What This Is

This repository contains the marketing website for **Willow Care Co.**, a nurse-led care navigation and patient advocacy business based in Florida.

The site is a single-file HTML/CSS/JS landing page — no framework, no build step, no dependencies. Open it in a browser and it works.

---

## The Business

Shelby has spent 20+ years inside every corner of healthcare — surgery suites, emergency rooms, geriatric wards, pediatric clinics, occupational health, and case management. The problem she kept encountering wasn't clinical. It was a communication failure.

Doctors speaking in acronyms. Discharge papers nobody explained. Families making decisions they didn't fully understand. Adult children juggling their own lives while trying to be their parent's advocate, translator, and coordinator all at once.

**Willow Care Co. fills that gap.**

Not as a home health agency. Not as a medical provider. As a nurse who shows up for your family — attends appointments, decodes care plans, builds personalized health packages, and makes sure nothing falls through the cracks between the diagnosis and the follow-up, between the hospital and home, between what the doctor said and what the family actually understood.

---

## Who It's For

- **Adult children managing aging parents** — especially from a distance
- **The sandwich generation** — balancing kids and parents simultaneously
- **Families navigating a new diagnosis** — who need someone to explain what's ahead

| Service | Description |
