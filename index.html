The Problem We Solve

The system moves fast.
Families get left behind.

Your parent gets discharged with a stack of papers. The doctor spoke for six minutes. The specialist appointment is in three weeks and nobody explained what happens until then.

You're juggling your job, your kids, and your own life — while trying to make sense of medication changes, referral networks, and care plans written in language designed for clinicians, not families.

That's where we come in. Willow Care Co. is a nurse-led navigation service. We don't replace your parent's medical team. We make sure your family can actually work with them.

What We Offer

Tailored care packages,
built around your family.

Every engagement is custom. No two care packages look the same because no two families — and no two patients — are the same.

🏥

Appointment Navigation

We attend physician visits with your loved one, take detailed notes, translate clinical language into plain English, and debrief the family after.

📋

Personalized Care Packages

A complete health binder: diagnosis summaries, medication lists, specialist contacts, care timelines, and action steps — all in one organized place.

🚗

Discharge & Transition Support

Hospitals discharge fast. We make sure your family knows exactly what happens next — follow-up care, warning signs, and who to call.

👥

Specialist Coordination

We bridge the gap between your primary provider and the specialists — ensuring everyone has the information they need to care for your loved one.

🌿

Senior Patient Advocacy

For aging parents managing chronic conditions, we serve as a consistent, knowledgeable presence who tracks changes, flags concerns, and keeps families informed.

💼

Employer Wellness Consulting

For small businesses, we build customized employee wellness programs rooted in clinical experience and occupational health expertise.

Your Navigator

Meet
Shelby.

After two decades working inside hospitals, clinics, surgery suites, and emergency rooms — Shelby knew what families needed most wasn't another provider. It was someone who could help them understand the ones they already had.

She built Willow Care Co. on a simple belief: clinical knowledge should serve families, not intimidate them. Her background spans geriatrics, surgical care, pediatrics, family practice, occupational health, and case management — which means she's seen your situation from nearly every angle.

She operates entirely within her licensed scope, working alongside your medical team — never around them. Think of her as the trusted family friend who happens to know exactly what your doctor meant.
